### EC2 Pricing Options Notes

### On-Demand
- Pay per use
- No commitment
- Most flexible
- Highest cost

### Reserved Instances (correct)
- 1 or 3 year commitment
- Lower cost
- Good for predictable workloads

### Spot Instances
- Cheapest
- Can be interrupted
- Good for batch / non-critical

### Dedicated Hosts
- Physical isolation
- Compliance / licensing
- Most expensive
